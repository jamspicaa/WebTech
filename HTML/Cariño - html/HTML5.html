HTML5

 Arose from the effort to evolve HTML (instead of replacing it) to address backward compatibility issues that hindered the adoption of XHTML, as well as to address the emerging needs of modern web applications.

 Work on HTML5 was initially undertaken by WHATWG (led by Apple, Mozilla and Opera) in 2004, with the W3C was published in 2008 (edited by Ian Hickson).

 HTML5 incorporated specifications from HTML 4, XHTML1, and DOM, and included various extensions of existing API’s and introduced new API’s.
